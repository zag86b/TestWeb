After calculation, you'll have the option to download a PDF report containing your results and the accompanying chart.

How to use this tool:

  1. **Enter Your Data**: Fill in the "Start Value," "End Value," and "Number of Periods" for each of the three scenarios you wish to compare.
  2. **Specify Periods**: Enter the number of **Years** and/or **Months**. For example, for 18 months, you can enter "1" year and "6" months, or "0" years and "18" months.
  3. **Leave Scenarios Blank**: If you only want to compare one or two scenarios, simply leave the input fields for the unused scenarios empty.
  4. **Calculate**: Click the "Calculate CAGR" button to see the annualized return for each scenario and view the comparative growth chart.
  5. **Download**: Once calculated, the "Download PDF" button will become active, allowing you to save your results and chart.
